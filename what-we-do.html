How We Serve

Real help for real relationships.

Enspire Global serves couples, families, men, women, churches, and ministry partners through practical coaching, events, discipleship, and storytelling — locally and around the world.

What We Offer

Six ways we serve — one mission.

Whether you're a couple looking for Christian marriage coaching, a pastor wanting a marriage event speaker for your church, a man seeking accountability and discipleship, a woman longing for community and encouragement, or a ministry needing visual storytelling — there's a place for you here.

01 Marriage
Events
For Churches & Couples

Bringing marriage ministry to your church.

Billy and Rebecca partner with churches as keynote speakers and ministry partners for marriage conferences, weekend retreats, date nights, and one-day events. They serve churches both large and small, coming alongside them to encourage and equip couples at every stage of relationship.

Whether your church is hosting its first marriage event or has an established program, Enspire Global will show up prepared, personal, and ready to serve your congregation well.

Invite Us to Your Church Ask a Question
02 Marriage
Coaching
For Couples, Men & Women

Wherever you are, we'll meet you there.

Billy and Rebecca coach couples together. Billy meets one-on-one with men. Rebecca meets one-on-one with women. However you come — as a couple fighting for your marriage, a man working on himself, or a woman needing someone in your corner — there's a place for you here.

Coaching sessions are available in person or virtually. They typically begin with an assessment to identify areas of strength and growth, followed by multiple sessions walking the couple through practical tools, conversation guides, and encouragement tailored to their specific needs.

20+ years of combined ministry experience  ·  Biblical principles-based  ·  Certified Adventures in Marriage facilitators  ·  10 years with FamilyLife

Note: Billy and Rebecca are not licensed counselors. When a couple needs care beyond coaching, they refer to trusted licensed professionals.

03 Men's
Ministry
For Men

Calling men out. Calling men up.

A major focus of Enspire Global is encouraging and equipping men to become who God has called them to be. Through Elevate, Billy meets regularly with men to help them grow in their faith, relationships, leadership, marriage, fatherhood, purpose, and courage.

His heart is to call men out of passivity and comfort — and into the purpose, leadership, and faith God has for them. Elevate also hosts a father/son retreat each fall and is developing an annual men's retreat.

Get Connected with Elevate
04 Women's
Discipleship
For Women

You are seen. You are loved. You are not alone.

Rebecca encourages and disciples women who are weary, struggling, or in need of a voice that points them back to the Lord. Her heart is to help women feel seen, strengthened, and encouraged in their walk with God and in the relationships and responsibilities He has placed in their lives.

Whether you're navigating a difficult season, feeling isolated, or wanting to grow deeper in your faith — Rebecca would love to come alongside you.

Connect with Rebecca
05 Visual
Storytelling
For Ministries & Organizations

Your story deserves to be told well.

Through Blindspot Media, Billy partners with ministries and organizations around the world to capture and share the stories of what God is doing — through photography and video that moves people to act, give, and believe.

From international mission trips to local church events, Blindspot Media helps ministries communicate their mission powerfully and authentically. Because the stories God is writing deserve more than a phone camera and a caption.

Visit Blindspot Media Work With Us
06 International
Missions
Around the World

Where God leads, we go.

Since launching Enspire Global in 2024, Billy and Rebecca have traveled internationally to serve alongside Christ-centered organizations in multiple countries — speaking at conferences, supporting marriage ministry, and capturing stories through Blindspot Media.

International missions is not a side project — it's woven into the DNA of Enspire Global. The same heart that shows up for a couple in Little Rock shows up in Kenya, Honduras, Peru, and beyond.
