Privacy Policy

How Enspire Global collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last Updated: June 2026

This privacy policy ("policy") will help you understand how Enspire Global ("us", "we", "our") uses and protects the data you provide to us when you visit and use enspireglobal.org ("website", "service").

We reserve the right to change this policy at any given time, of which you will be promptly updated. If you want to make sure you are up to date with the latest changes, we advise you to frequently visit this page.

What User Data We Collect

When you visit this website, we may collect the following data:

  • Your IP address
  • Your contact information and email address
  • Other information such as your name and how you heard about us

Why We Collect Your Data

We are collecting your data for several reasons:

  • To better understand your needs
  • To improve our ministry and resources
  • To send you promotional texts or emails containing information we think you will find interesting
  • To respond to your inquiries and prayer requests

Safeguarding and Securing Your Data

Enspire Global is committed to securing your data and keeping it confidential. Enspire Global has done all in its power to prevent data theft, unauthorized access, and disclosure by implementing the latest technologies and software, which help us safeguard all the information we collect online.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent information will not be shared with any third parties.

Analytics and Cookies

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information such as page views, time spent on pages, general geographic location (city/region level), device type, and browser type. This data is aggregated and anonymous — it does not personally identify you.

Google Analytics uses cookies to track sessions and returning visitors. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by disabling cookies in your browser settings.

This site may also use basic functional cookies. By using our website, you agree to allow our website to use cookies in this way.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links that lead to other websites. If you click on these links, Enspire Global is not responsible for your data and privacy protection. Visiting any other website is not governed by this privacy policy. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of any website you visit from ours.

Sharing Your Information

Enspire Global will not lease, sell, or distribute your personal information to any third parties, unless we have your permission or as required by law.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at info@enspireglobal.org.
