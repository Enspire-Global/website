Why We Go

The same heart that shows up in Little Rock shows up in Kenya.

International missions is not a side project for Enspire Global — it is woven into the DNA of who Billy and Rebecca are and what they believe God has called them to do.

Whether it's sitting with a pastor in Uganda, filming a mission team in the Dominican Republic, or capturing the stories of gospel workers in Colombia and Peru — the heart is always the same: to serve, to strengthen, and to tell the stories of what God is doing around the world.

"We go not because we have to, but because the gospel compels us — and because every story worth telling deserves to be told well."

Through Enspire Global and Blindspot Media, Billy brings professional photography and videography to ministries that need it most — at no charge, asking only that the cost of the trip be covered. It's a simple offer with a kingdom-sized impact.

Blindspot Media

Your ministry's story, told at no charge.

Through Blindspot Media, Billy travels internationally to produce professional photo and video content for ministries and organizations — completely free of charge. All he asks is that travel and trip expenses are covered.

The result is a high-quality visual record of your ministry's impact that you can use to inspire donors, recruit volunteers, and share the gospel with a wider audience.

If you lead a ministry or organization doing kingdom work around the world and you need someone to tell that story well — reach out.

Who We Serve Alongside

Ministry partners around the world.

A ministry of Cru focused on strengthening marriages and families. Billy and Rebecca served with FamilyLife for 10 years before launching Enspire Global.
