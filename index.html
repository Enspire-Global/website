Our Mission: Enspire Global is dedicated to helping individuals be equipped, encouraged, and empowered to live out their purpose, strengthen their families, and deepen their relationship with the Lord.

How We Serve

Equip. Encourage. Empower.

These three words are at the heart of everything Enspire Global does — for couples, families, men, women, and the communities around them.

Equip

We give couples, families, men, and women practical tools, coaching, and resources they can actually use — in their marriage, their home, and their walk with the Lord.

Encourage

Sometimes what people need most is someone to walk alongside them. We show up in the hard seasons, the drifting seasons, and the growing seasons — with honesty, warmth, and hope.

Empower

We help people step into who God created them to be. Whether it's a husband leading his family, a couple rebuilding trust, or a woman finding her footing — we call people out and call them up to elevate them to the life God has for them.

Our Story

From grief to calling — a decade of saying yes.

Billy and Rebecca Altman have been equipping and encouraging marriages and families for over a decade. After serving with FamilyLife, a ministry of Cru, for 10 years, they stepped out in faith in 2024 to launch Enspire Global.

"Lord, here I am. Use me." — the prayer that changed everything.

What began in a season of loss and surrender became a calling. Today, Billy and Rebecca pour into couples, men, women, and families — bringing practical tools, personal coaching, and the hope of the gospel to those who need it most.

How We Serve

Practical help for real relationships.

Enspire Global offers a range of ways to strengthen marriages, equip families, and encourage the people around you.

01

Marriage Events

We partner with churches as keynote speakers and ministry partners for conferences, weekend retreats, date nights, and one-day events — encouraging and equipping couples at every stage.

02

Marriage Coaching

One-on-one coaching for couples who want to grow stronger or need help rebuilding. Available in person or virtually — for any couple who reaches out.

03

Men's Ministry

Billy meets regularly with men to help them grow in faith, leadership, marriage, and purpose — calling them out and calling them up to elevate them to be the men God has called them to be.

04

Women's Discipleship

Rebecca encourages and disciples women who are weary, struggling, or in need of a voice that says: you are seen, you are loved, and you are not alone.

05

Visual Storytelling

Through photography and video, we partner with ministries around the world to capture and share stories of what God is doing — powerfully and beautifully.

06

International Missions

We travel globally to serve alongside Christ-centered organizations — speaking at conferences, supporting marriage ministry, and sharing the gospel through story.
