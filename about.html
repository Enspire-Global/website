Our Story

We didn't plan on being in ministry.
God had a better idea.

Billy and Rebecca Altman have given their lives to equipping, encouraging, and empowering marriages and families — and the story of how they got here is worth knowing.

"You are precious to me. You are honored, and I love you." Isaiah 43:4
How It Began

A loss that became a calling.

In 2010, Billy's father passed away suddenly from a heart attack. In the grief that followed, something unexpected happened — God began to heal Billy's heart and stir in him a willingness to surrender fully to whatever God wanted to do with his life.

Through that loss, Billy began to see how much of his energy had been poured into business, success, and providing — while the relationships closest to him needed deeper attention. He began to understand more fully how much people need to be pointed toward the Lord.

"One day, Rebecca came to him and said, 'I need my husband back.' That moment became a turning point."

Billy went out and sought the Lord, praying words that changed the trajectory of everything: "Lord, here I am. Use me." In that season of surrender, he sensed God leading him — not into a traditional pastor role, but into something different: equipping people through coaching, discipleship, and walking alongside them in their relationships.

Billy and Rebecca sat down together to talk through what God might be calling them to, and there was confirmation in both of their hearts. They had already been leading a small group for young married couples and had seen firsthand the need for encouragement, support, and practical coaching. They had also seen through friends the pain and devastation of divorce.

2010

The year that started the journey toward full-time ministry

The Altmans

Meet Billy & Rebecca.

After 10 years serving with FamilyLife, a ministry of Cru, they stepped out in faith in 2024 to launch Enspire Global — a ministry uniquely shaped by their story, their gifts, and their calling.

Co-Founder & Director

Billy Altman

Billy brings a background in military service, business ownership, and farming — and more than a decade of ministry experience — to his work with couples, men, and families. His heart is to call people out of comfort and into the life God has for them.

Through Enspire Global, Billy and Rebecca lead marriage coaching and speak at marriage events together. He also leads men's discipleship through Elevate and international visual storytelling through Blindspot Media.

Marriage Coaching Elevate Men's Ministry International Missions
Co-Founder & Director

Rebecca Altman

Rebecca brings more than a decade of ministry experience to her work with couples, women, and families. She holds a degree in psychology and has devoted herself to being a stay-at-home and homeschool mom since their children were born — a role that has deepened her wisdom and her heart for the people she serves.

Rebecca and Billy lead marriage coaching and speak at marriage events together as a team. She also disciples women who are weary, struggling, or in need of a voice that says: you are seen, you are loved, and you are not alone.

The Altman Family

Rooted in Little Rock.
Reaching the world.

Billy and Rebecca have been married since 1997 and have four children, two daughters and two sons, who are deeply part of the story God is writing through their family.

Enspire Global is based in Little Rock, Arkansas, and serves couples, families, churches, and ministry partners nationally and globally. They are not formally tied to a specific denomination, which allows them to serve freely across the church.

For Billy and Rebecca, family isn't separate from the calling. It is the calling.

Ministry Roots

Built on a decade of faithful service.

Before launching Enspire Global, Billy and Rebecca spent 10 years with FamilyLife, a ministry of Cru — one of the leading marriage and family ministries in the world. That foundation shapes everything they do today.
