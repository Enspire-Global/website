Why Give

You are not just supporting a ministry.
You are changing a generation.

Enspire Global is a faith-based ministry that depends entirely on the generosity of people who believe that marriages matter, families are worth fighting for, and the gospel needs to be proclaimed — here and around the world.

When you give to Enspire Global, your dollars go directly to work — funding coaching for couples who cannot afford it, covering the cost of marriage events, supporting international missions trips, and creating free resources for families everywhere.

There is no overhead-heavy organization between your gift and the work. Billy and Rebecca are in the field, serving people personally, and your giving keeps them there.

Ready to give?

Giving is safe, simple, and processed securely through Global Service Network. Every gift — large or small — makes a real difference.

Give a Gift Today

When you click Give, you'll be taken to our secure giving partner — Global Service Network. Your gift goes directly to supporting Enspire Global.

Your Gift at Work

What your giving makes possible.

Every dollar given to Enspire Global goes toward one of these six areas of ministry.

Marriage Coaching

Provide one-on-one coaching for couples who want to grow stronger or need help rebuilding — including couples who cannot afford it.

Marriage Events

Help churches host marriage conferences, date nights, and retreats that bring couples together and strengthen families in local congregations.

Men's Discipleship

Fund Elevate — the men's ministry that meets weekly to call men out of passivity and into the purpose, leadership, and faith God has for them.

International Missions

Cover the cost of international trips where Billy serves alongside missionaries — photographing, filming, speaking, and spreading the gospel around the world.

Free Resources

Create and distribute free tools, guides, and resources for couples, men, women, and families who need practical help but cannot pay for it.

Blindspot Media

Equip Billy to travel internationally and produce professional photo and video for ministries at no charge — covering only the cost of the trip.
